What is RetainMe?

RetainMe is a decision-support system equipped with machine learning algorithms. It predicts employee retention, acting as a pivotal resource for job applicant screening and evaluating their potential for long-term roles within organizations. By aiming to optimize the recruitment process, RetainMe ensures candidates have a higher likelihood of contributing to an organization's long-term objectives.

How Does It Work?

Job applicants register on RetainMe, completing their profiles with essential information. Following potential tests and surveys, their profile undergoes verification. Upon verification, RetainMe instantly predicts the likelihood of the applicant staying long-term if recruited. This prediction becomes an influential factor in recruitment, allowing for data-driven decision-making.

System Limitations

  • Our findings are primarily based on Emergence Systems and Solutions IT department, potentially making it less applicable to other industries.
  • Predictive models from past employee data might not represent future workforce dynamics accurately.
  • Our study is limited to classification algorithms, other techniques might offer different insights.
  • RDSS focuses on predicting retention for applicants, not current employees, limiting its applicability in ongoing employment scenarios.
  • External factors like political or economic events aren't considered in our retention rate analysis.
